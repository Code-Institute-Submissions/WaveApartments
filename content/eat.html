Eat & Drink

The North Sea provides a rich offering of various sea food delicacies. You can have mouth watering flounder fish or freshly caught brown shrimp tucked into a typical bread roll. Looking away from the sea you can enjoy meat from the surrounding farms. One of the specialities in the region is the salt marsh lamb. Nordfriesen, thats what the locals are called, also have a sweet tooth and you can find excellent cakes and bakerey goods in and around Sankt Peter Ording. To end the day you can sit at the beach and enjoy high quality cocktails while looking at the sunset.

Coffee & Tea

In Germany it is very typical to go out for "Kaffee & Kuchen" at around 2.30/3.00 pm. Below you will find the spots that will make you want to cry when trying there delicious cakes.

Schweizer Haus Fresh Cake, Art and Nature.

The "Schweizer Haus" is situated in Tating, which is only 5min by car from SPO. They serve delicious and big cake pieces accompanied by fine tea selections, hot chocolate or flavor rich coffee... schweizerhaus-tating.de

Das Strandhaus Cozy little oasis.

Very cozy atmosphere. Delicious coffee. Calorie-dense treats. Do you need more to have a good time? I don't think so. There is no SPO visit where we do not stop at the place... strandhaus-spo.de


Food

Being right at the sea is a guarantee for great sea food. But besides sea food SPO can surprise any guest and any preferance! From vegan to raw meat eater - everyone will find there place to be. Below are our highest recommendations...

Strandbar 54 Grad Nord Up in the sky.

Strandbar 54 Grad Nord is right on the beach. Whats amazing is the fact that the restaurant does not care about high tide or low tide. You will find yourself 7 Meters above the ground while enjoying a delicious lunch, a cold beer or hot mulled wine... strandbar-54grad-nord.de
