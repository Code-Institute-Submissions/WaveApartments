Hear the Sea & feel Relaxed

St. Peter Ordung (SPO) is situated on the peninsula Eiderstedt in Nordfriesland with direct access to the North Sea. The beaches and views are endless and the area offers options for any type of traveler.
However the weather - there is always something to do or explore.
